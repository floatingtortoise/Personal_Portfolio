genre: poem


An Ordinary Fabric

Saturday morning, you weave the unspoken words into stitches,
as usual. Under your hands, the needles pry open the mouths
of the two yarns -- I am one of them.
Breathe in. Breathe out. Then come closer. Like tango dancers
the two yarns come closer, until the fuzzy gap is gone.
So we’re woven into one piece under your hands
and mine, while our finger tips
remain three feet away fumbling, heedless of the covert birth of
a smouldering coal
in the push-and-pulls
Like another Saturday morning in the backyard
how our gazes grow into a vine
and climb up the roof in silence.
fall off   rotten              muse on death



一块普通织物

星期六的早上，你把未说出口的话织成布，
一如既往。在你的手里，棒针撬开了两根毛线
的嘴 -- 我是其中一根。
吸气，吐气，然后靠近。像探戈舞者般
两根毛线靠近，慢慢地，直到那朦胧的空隙消失。
就这样，在两双手下，我们被织成了一整片。
而我们的指尖
保持着一米远的距离，笨拙地工作，浑然不知
一团炊火隐秘地出生
在 拉 拽 之 间。
就如同
另一个星期六早上的后院里
我们的眼神长成一条藤蔓，在一片寂静之中
爬上屋顶。
脱落   腐烂       思索着死去
